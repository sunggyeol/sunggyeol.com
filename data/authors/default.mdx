Sung (Sunggyeol) Oh is a Computer Science student at Virginia Tech with a 3.91 GPA. His research spans the Tech on the Trail Lab, where he fine-tunes large language models for educational platforms, and the IDEEAS Lab, where he applied NLP to analyze social media sentiment toward generative AI in CS education. This work led to a first-author full paper at IEEE FIE 2024 and an extended abstract presented at CHI 2025.

Previously, as a software engineering intern at Fasoo, Sung developed a real-time speech-to-text system using WebSockets and Voice Activity Detection (VAD), significantly improving transcription speed and accuracy. He is skilled in Java, Python, TypeScript, AWS, and machine learning frameworks.
